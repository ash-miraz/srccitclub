About the Club

Our Story

The IT Club of SRCC is dedicated to fostering a tech-driven environment where students can explore their potential in coding, robotics, and design. We bridge the gap between academic learning and industry standards.

Our Mission

To create a platform where students can innovate and grow together.

Our Community

Connecting passionate tech enthusiasts within our college.
